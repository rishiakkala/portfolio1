# Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview
The product is a **personal portfolio website** for an AI/ML engineer and researcher. The website’s primary goal is to clearly communicate the individual’s identity, skills, experience, and credibility, while leaving a strong first impression through a thoughtfully designed hero section.

The site should feel **modern, professional, and human**, balancing technical depth with approachability.

---

## 2. Goals & Objectives
- Establish a strong personal brand and professional identity
- Showcase technical expertise, research, and publications
- Provide recruiters and collaborators with quick, clear access to information
- Differentiate from generic portfolios using intentional motion and clean design

---

## 3. Target Users
- Recruiters and hiring managers
- Research collaborators and academics
- Engineers and peers
- Conference reviewers or mentors

---

## 4. Key User Needs
- Quickly understand who the person is and what they do
- Assess technical skills and depth of experience
- Verify credibility through education and publications
- Easily initiate contact

---

## 5. Functional Requirements

### Hero Section
- Display name, role, and professional tagline
- Show a 2D avatar image prominently
- Background animation with an F1 car using smooth, bounded motion
- Subtle tire-track visuals that fade over time
- Animation must not obstruct text or avatar

### About Section
- Short narrative describing mindset, interests, and focus areas
- Clear, readable layout with minimal distractions

### Tech Skills Section
- Group skills by categories (systems, ML, data, tools)
- Present skills clearly without progress bars or ratings

### Education Section
- Timeline-based representation of academic background

### Experience Section
- Chronological list of professional or internship experiences
- Bullet points focused on outcomes and impact

### Publications Section
- List of research papers with title, venue, and year

### Get in Touch Section
- Email address with copy-to-clipboard functionality
- Links to GitHub and LinkedIn

---

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements
- Fast load times and smooth performance
- Accessible and readable on all screen sizes
- Motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
- SEO-friendly structure

---

## 7. Success Metrics
- Increased recruiter engagement
- Longer average session duration
- Positive qualitative feedback on design and clarity
