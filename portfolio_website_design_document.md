# Design Document

## 1. Design Vision
The design aims to communicate **engineering maturity, clarity, and human warmth**. The interface should feel intentional and calm, avoiding visual noise while still being memorable.

The hero section introduces motion and metaphor; subsequent sections slow the pace and focus on content clarity.

---

## 2. Visual Style
- **Theme:** Light, modern, minimal
- **Mood:** Calm, confident, professional
- **Inspiration:** Editorial layouts, modern SaaS landing pages, clean portfolio designs

---

## 3. Color Palette
- Primary background: #F9FAFB
- Section background: #F1F3F5
- Primary text: #1F2933
- Secondary text: #4B5563
- Accent color: #C06A3E (warm copper)
- Link accent: #3B82F6

---

## 4. Typography
- Font family: Inter / Satoshi (or similar)
- Headings: Semi-bold to bold
- Body text: Regular weight, generous line height
- No decorative or display fonts

---

## 5. Layout Structure

### Hero Section
- Two-column layout
- Left: Name, role, tagline
- Right: 2D avatar with soft radial glow
- Canvas-based background animation (F1 car and tire tracks)

### About Section
- Two-column layout
- Text-focused with minimal imagery
- Thin accent line for structure

### Tech Skills
- Grid or stacked layout
- Skills grouped by category
- Hover underline interaction

### Education
- Vertical timeline
- Thin accent line and simple nodes

### Experience
- Stacked content blocks
- Clear hierarchy of role, organization, and impact

### Publications
- Minimal list format
- Emphasis on readability and credibility

### Get in Touch
- Centered layout
- Friendly call-to-action
- Icon links and email

---

## 6. Motion & Interaction Guidelines
- Motion should feel purposeful and restrained
- No looping animations outside the hero section
- Use opacity and small transforms for transitions
- All motion must be optional based on user preference

---

## 7. Accessibility & Responsiveness
- High contrast text
- Keyboard navigable
- Fully responsive across devices
